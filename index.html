What We're Changing & Why

A complete redesign of the Checkr Sales Onboarding program for 2026

The previous onboarding model front-loaded all content into a two-week remote sprint with no certification framework, no structured manager involvement, and no post-bootcamp reinforcement. This redesign addresses all three problems — creating a year-long learning journey grounded in practice, accountability, and just-in-time delivery.
What We're Changing
BeforeAfter
2-week front-loaded remote bootcamp → A year-long learning journey — pre-work, 5-day in-person bootcamp, and structured milestones at 30, 90, 180, and 365 days.
Lecture-heavy Zoom sessions → Bootcamp built entirely around workshops, role-plays, and activities. Reps complete foundational content in pre-work so every in-person hour is practice.
No formal certification → Five certifications tracked in Mindtickle: cold call, discovery, demo, pricing, and written product. Managers see scores automatically and in real time.
Role-agnostic content → Split sessions by role (BDR / Commercial AE / Enterprise AE) where content diverges. Every rep gets training directly relevant to their book and quota.
Manager involvement optional → Managers are a required part of the program — 30/90/180/365-day check-ins, weekly Gong reviews, and account list approval before live outreach begins.
Industry overviews skipped or rushed → Industry modules in pre-work (overview) and post-bootcamp (deep dive). Reps speak the language of every vertical from Day 1 of bootcamp.
Expected Impact on New Hires
Faster ramp time
Reps arrive at bootcamp already knowing the product and methodology. First outbound activity begins the week after bootcamp.
Higher call confidence
Every call type is practiced at least 3 times — role-play, calibration, and certification — before a rep touches a real prospect.
Clearer expectations
Reps know exactly what is expected at 30, 45, 90, 180, and 365 days. Milestones are documented in Mindtickle and reviewed with managers.
Better industry fluency
Industry overviews in pre-work and deep dives post-bootcamp mean reps speak the language of the buyer from Day 1 — not just Checkr features.
Stronger manager relationships
Required check-ins and weekly Gong reviews create a structured coaching rhythm that benefits both rep and manager in the critical first 90 days.

The Three Phases
Phase 1

Pre-Work

Day 2 of employment · 4 days × 4 hours = 16 hours

Enablement Welcome ILT on Day 2 (Tuesday morning)
10 self-paced Mindtickle modules across Days 2–6
Product, ICP, CoM, competitors, outbound, and call listening
Must complete before Bootcamp Day 1
Product 101 (~5 hrs) + Industry Overviews (~4–5 hrs) are the heaviest days
Phase 2

Bootcamp

San Francisco or Denver · Monday–Friday

Day 1: Context & Foundation — pains, ICP, competitors, CoM
Day 2: Practicing the Conversations — cold call & discovery role-plays
Day 3: How We Sell — overcoming inertia, demo & pricing role-plays
Day 4: Outbound Motion — account research, messaging, sequencing
Day 5: Increasing Productivity — tools, deal mechanics, legal, wrap-up
Phase 3

Post-Bootcamp

Week 1 through Year 1 · Coaching + modules + milestones

Live outreach begins Week 3
Advanced industry deep dives (Weeks 3–4, Month 2)
Weekly Gong call reviews with manager
Bi-weekly Homeroom modules (ongoing, required)
Check-ins at 30, 45, 90, 180, and 365 days
Manager-assigned Checkpoint Missions based on Gong gaps
