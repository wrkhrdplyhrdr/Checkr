Day 1 to Year 1

Three phases from first day of employment through full productivity. Pre-Work and Bootcamp build the foundation — everything after is reinforcement, coaching, and ramp.

The Three Phases
Phase 1
Pre-Work
Days 1–5 of employment · ~28 hours
HR / IT / NHO orientation + Manager 1:1
Tools setup + Sales Tools Overview (all 10 tools)
Product 101 (~5 hrs) + ICP & Personas
Industry Overviews — all 5 verticals
CoM 101 + Competitors + Call Overviews
Gong call library — 1–2 calls/day
Phase 2
5-Day Bootcamp
SF or Denver · Monday–Friday
Day 1: Context & Foundation — pains, ICP, competitors, Fireside
Day 2: Conversations — CoM, cold call, discovery, Gong
Day 3: How We Sell — legal, demo, pricing, SE partnership
Day 4: Outbound — account research, partners, events, messaging
Day 5: Productivity — Athean, SCOUT, Jeopardy, wrap-up
Phase 3
Post-Bootcamp
Week 1 through Year 1 · Coaching + milestones
Weeks 3–4: 5 call certifications (cold, disco, demo, pricing, narrative)
Month 1: First pipeline, MEDDPICC on all opps, 30-day review
Month 2: Advanced industry modules, 45-day pipeline review
Month 3: 90-day milestone — quota ramp begins
Month 6 & Year 1: Proficiency and performance reviews
