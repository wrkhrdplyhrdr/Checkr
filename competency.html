Competency Framework

The 8 Core Competencies

What great looks like at Checkr — grounded in the January 2026 cohort. Each competency maps pre-work, bootcamp, and post-bootcamp reinforcement. Click a competency to expand its learning arc.

01
Product Knowledge
What we sell and how to teach it to a prospect
›

Pre-Work

  • Product 101 (~5 hours, Days 3–4)
  • Covers: packages & add-ons, criminal, MVR/DoT, candidate experience, international, IDV, assess & adjudication, verifications, drug & occ health, professional services, dashboard & analytics

Bootcamp

Product & Industry Fireside Panel

Product 101 is completed in pre-work. The Day 1 Fireside Panel brings PMMs in to answer questions that surfaced during pre-work — submitted by reps in Mindtickle before bootcamp begins.

Post-Bootcamp

  • Company Narrative Certification — Weeks 3–4 (3–5 min, persona-specific, no notes)
  • Homeroom product update modules (bi-weekly, ongoing)
  • Manager spot-checks product knowledge via Gong call reviews
  • Advanced product questions answered via Checkpoint Seller Copilot
02
ICP & Persona Fluency
Who we sell to and why it matters
›

Pre-Work

  • ICP & Personas 101
  • Industry Overviews — All 5 Verticals (~4–5 hours, Days 4–5)
  • Review: Oscar in Ops, Helen in HR, Travis in TA persona PDFs
  • Review: ICP hubs in Checkpoint for each vertical

Bootcamp

Review: Pains and Personas

A follow-up to the pre-work ICP & Personas 101 module. Reps apply the framework with a live exercise: map pain triggers to specific personas for accounts in their territory.

Post-Bootcamp

  • Advanced Retail deep dive — Checkpoint e-learning (Weeks 3–4)
  • Advanced Hospitality deep dive — Checkpoint e-learning (Weeks 3–4)
  • Advanced Manufacturing deep dive — Checkpoint e-learning (Month 2)
  • Advanced Transportation deep dive — Checkpoint e-learning (Month 2)
  • Advanced Staffing deep dive — Checkpoint e-learning (Month 2)
  • ABM campaign Homeroom modules aligned to active industry campaigns
  • Persona/Trigger Activity — completed in bootcamp Day 2 · Rep continues applying in live outreach Week 3+
  • Manager check: rep articulates 3 pain points per ICP industry by Month 1
