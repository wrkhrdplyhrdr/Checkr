Weeks 4–5: Certifications

Five Mindtickle certifications to complete in Weeks 4–5, after bootcamp. These are recorded and scored by your manager against a rubric. Pass score is 80%. Live calls begin after all five are passed.

✅
Certification Review
Manager scores and debriefs each certification within 48 hours
Your manager reviews your recordings and provides written feedback. Any cert scored below 80% requires a re-record. Live calls begin after all 5 certs are passed.
☎
Effective Communication — Cold Call
Cold Call Certification
What I need to demonstrate
  • A persona-specific opening hook that names the Before Scenario in the first 15 seconds
  • Handle the top 3 objections using the AAA method without breaking rhythm
  • Secure a clear next step before ending the call
  • Talk ratio under 60% — I ask, they talk
How I’m assessed
  • Record a live or simulated cold call in Mindtickle
  • Manager scores against the cold call rubric
  • Pass = 80% or above on all rubric dimensions
  • Retake available if below 80%
